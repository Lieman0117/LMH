When GoDaddy Is the Better Choice

GoDaddy has real advantages in certain situations. It is the better option when:

  • You need a simple placeholder page live in under an hour at minimal cost
  • You are already a GoDaddy customer managing your domain there and want everything in one account
  • Your business does not depend on appearing in local Google search results
  • You want 24/7 phone support from a large team without a premium price tag
  • You are not yet ready to invest in a professional website and just need something live temporarily
Common Questions

LMH vs GoDaddy FAQ

  • Is GoDaddy Website Builder good for a trade business?

    For a simple placeholder, it works. But GoDaddy's template builder typically scores 50–70 on Google Lighthouse performance, which can hurt rankings in competitive local searches. A hand-coded site is a significantly better foundation for a trade business that relies on local search traffic.

  • Does GoDaddy include hosting?

    Yes, GoDaddy Website Builder plans include hosting. LMH Web Solutions also includes hosting, along with custom hand-coded design, SSL, ongoing edits, and support, all in the $175/month subscription.

  • Can I move from GoDaddy to LMH Web Solutions?

    Yes. We build your new site while your GoDaddy site remains live, then transfer the domain once ready. If your domain is registered with GoDaddy, it transfers with you. You keep it regardless.

  • What is the main difference between GoDaddy and LMH Web Solutions?

    GoDaddy Website Builder is DIY template software. LMH Web Solutions is a done-for-you service: we hand-code every site from scratch, guarantee 95+ Lighthouse scores, and include ongoing edits and support in one flat monthly price.

When Squarespace Is the Better Choice

We think hand-coded wins for tradespeople, but Squarespace genuinely is the better fit in these situations:

  • You want full DIY control over your own design without paying a developer
  • You are testing a business idea and need something live in a few hours for under $20/month
  • Your site is primarily a portfolio or blog, not a lead-generation tool for a service area
  • You enjoy designing and updating your own website as a hobby
  • You do not need industry-specific design or local SEO optimization
Common Questions

LMH vs Squarespace FAQ

  • Is LMH Web Solutions better than Squarespace for electricians?

    For US electricians who want a fast, search-optimized website without managing it themselves, LMH Web Solutions is the better fit. Squarespace is better if you want full DIY control and a lower monthly spend.

  • Does Squarespace rank well on Google?

    Squarespace sites typically score 50–75 on Google Lighthouse performance due to template bloat and third-party scripts. Hand-coded sites from LMH Web Solutions consistently score 95+, which contributes to faster load times and better rankings in competitive local searches.

  • Can I move from Squarespace to LMH Web Solutions?

    Yes. We build your new hand-coded site while your Squarespace site stays live, then switch the domain once everything is ready. You keep your domain name throughout the transition.

  • Why is LMH Web Solutions more expensive than Squarespace?

    Squarespace is software. You pay for access and do all the design, content, and maintenance yourself. LMH Web Solutions is a done-for-you service: we handle design, development, hosting, edits, and support. The price difference reflects a fully managed website vs DIY software.

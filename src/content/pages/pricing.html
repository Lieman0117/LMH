Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

  • How long until my website is live?

    Most trade business websites are live within 5 to 10 business days from your first consultation. That includes design, development, revisions, and testing.

  • What is included in the monthly price?

    Your $175/month covers a fully custom hand-coded website, hosting, SSL, SEO-ready markup, contact forms, a blog/CMS, and ongoing support and content edits. No hidden fees.

  • Are there any setup fees?

    No. There is no setup fee, no design fee, and no onboarding charge. You pay $175/month starting from month one.

  • Do I own the code?

    The code is managed by LMH Web Solutions as part of your subscription. You own your domain name and all your content. If you cancel, you keep your domain and we will help you transition smoothly.

  • What happens if I cancel?

    Cancel any month with no penalty. There are no long-term contracts. You keep your domain and all your content. We will help you move to whatever comes next.

  • Do you do edits and updates after launch?

    Yes, ongoing content edits are included. Need to update your service area, change pricing, add a team member, or swap out photos? Just reach out and we will handle it.

  • What is not included?

    eCommerce functionality, paid advertising management, and photography are not included. We focus on building a fast, high-converting website, the lead generation foundation for your trade business.

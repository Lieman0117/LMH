Our Company

About LMH Web Solutions

LMH Web Solutions is an independent web studio, not affiliated with LMH Agency or Lokal Media House. We are a completely separate company with no connection to those organizations.

Founded in 2025 by Liam H., LMH Web Solutions builds websites exclusively for US tradespeople: electricians, painters, plumbers, and other skilled contractors. We exist because trade businesses deserve websites that perform, not templates that slow them down.

Every site we deliver is hand-coded from scratch. No Wix. No Squarespace. No page builders. That discipline is how we guarantee 95+ scores across every Google Lighthouse category: performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO, on every single build.

Our model is simple: one flat monthly subscription, no setup fee, no contracts. Hosting, SSL, support, and edits are all included. You focus on the job; we handle the website.

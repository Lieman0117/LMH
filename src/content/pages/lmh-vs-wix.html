When Wix Is the Better Choice

Wix has genuine strengths that make it the right call in specific situations:

  • You are an absolute beginner who wants drag-and-drop control with no technical knowledge required
  • Your budget is tight and you need something live for under $20/month that you manage yourself
  • You want to test a business concept before investing in a professional website
  • Your business does not rely heavily on local Google search traffic
  • You need a large library of third-party apps and integrations quickly
Common Questions

LMH vs Wix FAQ

  • Is Wix good for a plumber or electrician website?

    Wix can get a basic site live quickly, but its JavaScript-heavy framework typically scores 40–65 on Google Lighthouse performance. For trade businesses competing in local search, a hand-coded site will load faster and rank better.

  • What is Wix's Lighthouse performance score?

    Wix sites typically score between 40 and 65 on Google Lighthouse performance because the platform loads a large JavaScript framework on every page. LMH Web Solutions hand-coded sites consistently score 95–100 because there is no framework overhead.

  • Can I switch from Wix to LMH Web Solutions?

    Yes. We build your new site while your Wix site remains live, then transfer the domain once everything is ready. Your domain belongs to you. You are not locked in to Wix.

  • Does Wix own my website?

    You own your content on Wix, but the website cannot be exported. If you leave, you lose the site design. With LMH Web Solutions, you own your content and your domain always stays with you.
